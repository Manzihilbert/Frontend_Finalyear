Locations Where IDs can be found

College of Computing and Information Sciences (COCIS)

This college is located just above the main gate in Makerere University and it has two blocks. in case your ID has been deposited here. Go to block A and you will find a secretary at the very entrance and she will provide you with assistance in regards to getting your ID.

Mitchell Hall

This is a residence of male students at Makerere University. The easiest way to get there is to enter the University from the small gate and move straight. Take a left turn and move up the hill. On your right, you will see a building with a rat shaped monument. That is Mitchell Hall.

Complex Hall

This is a residence of female students at Makerere University. To get there, enter from the main gate take a left turn and enter the building on your right. There is a notice board on the first floor where you can get your ID.

Mary stuart Hall

This is a residence of female students at Makerere university, and to get there from the main gate take a long left turn until you see a very tall building enter opposite the reception there is a notice board where you can find your ID.
