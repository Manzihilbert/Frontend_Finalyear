Uploading a lost ID
Step 1

Find a misplaced Makerere University ID. Ensure you have an internet connection and then log into MakRecover and click on the upload ID button on your home screen.

Step 2

You will be taken to a page that gives an option to take a photo of the ID or upload a photo of it from you phone or computer storage.

Step 3

Choose the most favourable option and then select a photo to upload. of the ID.

Caution

Ensure the photo being uploaded is visible enough to ease the process of matching it to its user.

Step 4

Select a location where the owner of the ID will find it and this can be a university college, hall of residence or the university police station.

Step 5

After all the above steps have been followed, click on the upload button and the ID will be uploaded.
If the ID has been successfully uploaded, you will be redirected to a page telling you that the ID has been successfully uploaded.

Step 6

Thank you for using MakRecover!
