Searching for lost ID
Step 1

If you already have an account with a clear photo of yourself, click on the search button on your home page.

Step 2

Wait for the system do its magic.

Step 3

After the system has made a successful search for you ID and it has been found,
you will see a photo of an ID and a percentage of how much the system thinks the ID matches your photo.

Step 4

You will be requested to comfirm if the ID is yours or not. By clicking yes or no at the bottom of the page.

Step 5

When you click yes, you will receive an email to your registered email address with information about the place where you can ge your ID.

Step 5

Go to that location and pick up your ID.

ID not found

If you carry out the search and after step 2 you receive a message telling you that your ID has not been found, this means that you ID has not yet been uploaded to the system.

ID not found

if you urgently need a new ID, you can go the the FAQs page and follow to steps on how to get a new ID.
